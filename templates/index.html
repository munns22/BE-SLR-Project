What is SignLearn ?

Sign language is the basic means of communication for those with hearing and vocal disabilities. Those disadvantaged have difficulty in their day-to-day lives. We aim to develop a system that would ease this difficulty in communication. A Sign Language Recognition System using Neural Network algorithm and Image Processing. The system will detect hand gestures from images and give textual output and vice versa.
