About Us

Who we are :

Sign language is the basic means of communication for those with hearing and vocal disabilities. Those disadvantaged have difficulty in their day-to-day lives. We aim to develop a system that would ease this difficulty in communication.
The proposed systems can be very helpful for the deaf and dumb people in communicating with others ; as knowing sign language is not something that is common to all.

At the heart of all we do is our commitment to :

Millions of people around the world suffer from hearing disability. This large number demonstrates the importance of developing a sign language recognition system converting sign language to text; for sign language to become clearer, to understand without a translator.

Our project aims to bridge the gap between the speech and hearing impaired people and the normal people. Readable output, will make it easy to communicate. Our project also aims to create a two way communication for the users, i.e..: sign language to text and text to sign language.
